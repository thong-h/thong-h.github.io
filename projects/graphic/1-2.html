J&J Menu (2020)

DESCRIPTION:

For this project, I redesign the logo and make a trifold for the family restaurant that I usually come. It is the Hawaiian BBQ restaurant named J&J in Cupertino, near De Anza College. Their food is delicious and the service is great, too. However, the logo and the design for menu is not on point, so I decide to make a new version.

Tool: Illustrator, Indesign

SIZE: 11 x 8.5 in. (spread); 3.8675 x 8.5 in. (tri-fold)
