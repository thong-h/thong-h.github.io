Pride (2019)

DESCRIPTION:

This is a portrait poster of Neville Brody - an English graphic designer, typographer, and art director. The work includes his portrait, two of his artworks and a quote that intrigued me the most. The England flag as a background is to give the audience the information about him, and the color of the flag gives the nice contrast and helps highlighting the black and white portrait.

Tool: Illustrator

SIZE: 11 x 17 in.
