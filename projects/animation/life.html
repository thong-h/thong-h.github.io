LIFE Explainer Video (2023)

Description:

An animated explainer video for the company LIFE (Latinos In Film & Entertainment), which builds the Latino/Hispanic community to support all the members by linking people working in the industry.

The goal is to demonstrate to the community how simple it is to connect with members of the community in order to bridge the gap between real-world relevant occupations and what LIFE has to offer.

Software:

Illustrator, After Effects
