Into the Cathedral (2021)

DESCRIPTION:

- This a group project in which I played the role of 3D assets modeler and interaction designer.
- Into the Cathedral is a 3D horror game that focuses on world building design and interactivity. Here, you will explore the abandoned Cathedral that is in the middle of a post-apocalyptic setting, in short, an abandoned Cathedral where everything is broken, damaged, worn out. While exploring the place, look out for a monster hiding somewhere inside the ruin.
