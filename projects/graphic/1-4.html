The Myth of Icarus (2020)

Description:

The fictional association name is The Association of National Greece Education in Legends (ANGEL). ANGEL is looking to produce content about Ancient Greek myths.
They are a travel company, which provides tour guides and information, locate near the Acropolis Museum in Athens, Greece. Their clients are visitors who are interested in Greek Mythology. The mythology is about the escape of Icarus and Daedalus on the waxed wings.

Tool: Illustrator, InDesign

Size: 18 x 10 in. (spread), 6 x 10 in. (z-fold)
