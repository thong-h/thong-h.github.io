Present Time (2020)

DESCRIPTION:

Present Time is a video about a day of my life and things around me that reflect the crises that we are all going through because of the pandemic, the wildfire and so on. I used macro-len to give the audience a closer perspective of animals' life and how they interact with the environment. Thanks to the rapidity in movements and changing directions of the ants, I could create a moment when the animal sense the dangerous approaching them. The ants are humanity and the wave is the pandemic. The intensity reaches its climax when the water wave comes and wipes out the ants; just like the pandemic, it came suddenly with the deadly power that took lives away.

TOOL: Premiere Pro, Iphone [XR], Macro-len

QUALITY: FHD 1080
