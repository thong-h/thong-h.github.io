HiCha's Tea Packaging (2020)

Description:

The tea farm that I design for is called HiCha (pronounce “Hee – cha”) from Japan. It has fields of green tea, black tea, and ginger. The owner wants the logo to be eco-friendly, fresh, and the modern, appealing look in the packaging to help attract more customers, so I decide to make the tea packagings have the illustrations of the tea leaves and gingers; also, the main colors are green, black and brown gold in associated with three types of tea.

Software:

Illustrator

Size:

11.8 x 10.3 in. (open flat); 5 x 2.65 x 3 in. (box)
